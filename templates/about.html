About HNS ERP System

HNS ERP System is a lightweight yet powerful enterprise resource planning (ERP) platform designed for small to medium businesses. It streamlines core business operations, giving you full control over inventory, sales, purchases, and invoicing â€” all from a single, intuitive interface.

Key Features

  • Centralized Dashboard: View real-time summaries of inventory, sales, purchases, and stock valuation. Track key business metrics at a glance.
  • Inventory Management: Add, edit, and track all your products with ease. Monitor stock levels and automatically identify low-stock items. Set product prices and manage detailed descriptions.
  • Sales Management: Create and manage orders efficiently. Automatic deduction of sold items from inventory. Track total sales, customer orders, and recent transactions.
  • Purchase Management: Record supplier purchases and update inventory in real-time. Monitor total expenditure on purchases. Keep supplier information organized and accessible.
  • Invoice Generation & PDF Export: Generate professional invoices with your company name, PO number, and PO date. Download invoices as PDF instantly. Link invoice details to actual orders for accurate billing.
  • Reports & Analytics: View detailed sales and purchase summaries. Analyze stock value and movement trends. Access recent transactions to make informed decisions.
  • User Management & Security: Secure login system with user authentication. Option to create multiple users with controlled access. Built-in test user for quick setup and demonstration.
  • Easy Deployment & Accessibility: Web-based application accessible from any device with internet. Compatible with both local and cloud hosting (Render, Heroku, etc.). Lightweight and optimized for small to medium business operations.
  • Future-Ready & Extensible: Built with Flask and SQLAlchemy, making it easy to customize. Ready for integration with other business tools and modules. Designed to grow with your business needs.
