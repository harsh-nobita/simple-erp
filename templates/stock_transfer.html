How it Works

Stock Transfer Process:

  • Select the item to transfer
  • Choose source (From) warehouse
  • Choose destination (To) warehouse
  • Enter quantity to transfer
  • Optionally specify a batch number for FIFO transfer
  • Add transfer notes for tracking

Notes: Stock is automatically reduced from source warehouse and added to destination. Batch information is preserved.
